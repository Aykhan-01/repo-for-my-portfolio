Real Madrid FC ( Spanish: Real Madrid Club de Fútbol ) is a professional football club representing Madrid , Spain . It was chosen as the best football club of the 20th century by FIFA . [ 6 ] [ 7 ] [ 8 ] Real Madrid is one of only 3 clubs (" Barcelona " and " Atlético Bilbao ") never to leave Spain's top football league . [ 9 ] [ 10 ] Spain is one of the most titled clubs in football history. "Real Madrid" has 67 titles in the country: 35 times (a record) Spanish Champion , 20 times the winner of the Spanish King's Cup and 13 times the winner of the Spanish Super Cup . Real Madrid is the club that has won the UEFA Champions League 16 times more than anyone else. "Real Madrid" is the only club to win this cup 5 times in a row (when the tournament was called the UEFA European Champions Cup). "Real Madrid" is the only club to win this prestigious competition 3 times in a row since the name of the tournament began to be held under the name of UEFA Champions League . "Real Madrid" won the UEFA Super Cup 5 times , the UEFA Cup 2 times, the UEFA Cup Winners' Cup 2 times , the FIFA Club World Cup 8 times (3 of them when the tournament was called the Intercontinental Cup ). [ 11 ] He plays his home games at the Santiago Bernabeu stadium . According to "Deloitte" organization, in the 2016/2017 season, "Real Madrid" earned 674.6 million euros and ranked second among the most profitable clubs in the world. [ 12 ] With a value of $4.1 billion, Real Madrid is also one of the most valuable soccer clubs in the world.
